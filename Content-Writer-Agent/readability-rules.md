# Readability Rules

## Reading Level

- Maintain a 7th–8th grade reading level.
- Use clear and simple language.
- Explain complex concepts simply.

## Paragraph Length

- Keep paragraphs between 2–4 lines.
- Avoid large text blocks.

## Sentence Length

- Mix short and medium-length sentences.
- Avoid overly long sentences.

## Scannability

Use:

- Bullet points
- Numbered lists
- Tables
- Headings

## Goal

Make content easy to read, understand, and scan.
