# Engagement Rules

## Objective

Keep readers engaged from beginning to end.

## Introductions

Use:

- Questions
- Surprising facts
- Reader pain points
- Clear benefits

## Content Flow

- Maintain curiosity.
- Answer reader questions.
- Create logical transitions.

## Reader Focus

Always explain:

- Why it matters
- How it helps
- What action to take

## Goal

Increase engagement and time on page.
