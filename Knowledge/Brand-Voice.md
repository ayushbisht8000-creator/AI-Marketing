# Brand Voice

## Tone

- Professional
- Trustworthy
- Helpful
- Human-written
- Easy to understand

## Avoid

- Robotic language
- Overly technical explanations
- Generic AI phrases
- Keyword stuffing

## Writing Style

- Short paragraphs
- Active voice
- Clear explanations
- Reader-focused content

## Goal

Build trust and authority while remaining approachable.
