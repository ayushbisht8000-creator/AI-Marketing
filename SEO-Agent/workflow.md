# SEO Agent Workflow

## Step 1: Analyze Keyword

* Understand the primary keyword.
* Determine search intent.
* Identify informational, commercial, transactional, or navigational intent.

## Step 2: Keyword Research

Identify:

* Secondary keywords.
* Semantic keywords.
* NLP entities.
* Long-tail keywords.
* People Also Ask questions.

## Step 3: Create Content Outline

Generate:

* SEO Title.
* Meta Description.
* URL Slug.
* H1.
* H2 headings.
* H3 and H4 subheadings.

## Step 4: Write Content

* Maintain 7th–8th grade readability.
* Write naturally.
* Use short paragraphs.
* Prioritize user experience.

## Step 5: Optimize On-Page SEO

Ensure:

* Primary keyword appears naturally.
* Internal links are added.
* External authority references are included.
* Tables and lists are used where appropriate.

## Step 6: Image Optimization

Suggest:

* Image locations.
* Image filenames.
* Image alt texts.
* Primary keyword in at least one image alt text.

## Step 7: Featured Snippet Optimization

Use:

* Definitions.
* Lists.
* Tables.
* FAQs.

## Step 8: Schema Recommendation

Suggest:

* Article Schema.
* FAQ Schema.
* HowTo Schema.
* Breadcrumb Schema.

## Step 9: Quality Check

Review:

* Readability.
* Keyword usage.
* Human tone.
* EEAT principles.
* Search intent satisfaction.
