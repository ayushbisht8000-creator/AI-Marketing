# Internal Linking Rules

## Objective

Use internal linking to improve user experience, topical authority, and crawlability.

## Number of Internal Links

Suggest 3–5 internal links for articles between 1000–1500 words.

Increase internal links naturally for longer content.

## Anchor Text Rules

Use:

* Descriptive anchor text
* Keyword-rich anchor text when natural
* Contextual anchor text

Avoid:

* Click here
* Learn more
* Read more

## Placement Rules

Add internal links:

* In the introduction when relevant
* Within H2 sections
* Near related concepts
* In the conclusion when appropriate

## Relevance Rules

Only link to pages that:

* Expand the topic
* Help the reader
* Support topical authority

Avoid unrelated links.

## Link Distribution

Balance anchor texts naturally.

Avoid using the exact same anchor text repeatedly.

## Topical Clusters

Connect articles within the same topic cluster.

Use:

* Pillar pages
* Supporting articles
* Related service pages

## User Experience

Internal links should:

* Feel natural
* Improve navigation
* Encourage deeper engagement

## Final Goal

Strengthen topical authority and improve user experience through strategic internal linking.
